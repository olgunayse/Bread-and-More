✦ Our Story

A little kitchen with a big heart

Bread and More started as a weekend passion — simit rings warming on the stone, sourdough starters bubbling on the counter, the kitchen filled with the scent of toasted sesame and crusty bread.

The name says it all. Every loaf carries a piece of that warmth — slow-fermented doughs, hand-shaped rings, nothing rushed. Just bread made the way it should be, and always a little something more.

We bake on Thursdays and Fridays for weekend collection, and take orders up to 48 hours in advance so every piece arrives fresh from the oven.
